# Product Overview

> Status: Early scoping — only the scope constraint is confirmed so far. Audience, positioning, and name are pending the discovery flow and will be filled in once the user provides them.

## What it is
A single landing page. Nothing more. The user's explicit ask: "the most minimum LP, just 1 page — do not add anything."

## Scope (confirmed)
- **Exactly one page.** No additional routes, sections, or sub-pages beyond what one landing page requires.
- **No extra features.** Do not add functionality the user did not ask for. Minimalism is the requirement, not a placeholder for "we'll add more later."

## Strategic principles
- Honor the minimalism. Restraint is the product decision here — resist scope creep.
- Keep it shippable fast. One clean page that does its job.

## Pending (to confirm via scoping)
- **Purpose** — what the landing page is for / what it promotes.
- **Audience** — who lands on it.
- **The one job** — the single thing the page must do brilliantly (e.g., capture a signup, explain an offer).
- **Name** — what to call it.
